## 📖 Project Overview
This project analyzes the fundamental relationship between **Economic Freedom** and **Global Prosperity**. By utilizing the **2022 Index of Economic Freedom** dataset from The Heritage Foundation, we developed a Tableau-based analytics suite to visualize how freedom in labor, property, and markets drives human progress and poverty reduction.

### **Key Highlights**
* **100% Logic Accuracy**: Fully verified correlations between Economic Freedom scores and prosperity indicators.
* **SDLC Compliance**: Followed a structured 7-phase development lifecycle: Ideation, Requirements, Design, Development, Testing, Deployment, and Demonstration.
* **Global Scope**: Comprehensive analysis of 184 countries across 12 quantitative and qualitative factors.

## 🛠️ Technical Features & Analytics
### **Core Visualizations**
1. **Global Freedom Pulse**: A geographic heat map ranking the Top 40 and Bottom-performing countries.
2. **Economic Correlations**: Advanced scatter plots analyzing the link between **Inflation vs. Unemployment** and **GDP (PPP) vs. Monetary Freedom**.
3. **The 4 Pillars**: Deep-dive analysis into Rule of Law, Government Size, Regulatory Efficiency, and Open Markets.

### **Performance Testing**
* **Workbook Optimization**: Achieved a high best-practice score (16/20) in the Tableau Workbook Optimizer.
* **Efficiency**: Minimized the **Amount of Data Rendered** and optimized **Filter Utilization** for high-speed web responsiveness.
